Ghoomar Village Gurugram FAQs

Comprehensive information about the Ghoomar Village experience, food, entertainment, and facilities.

What is Ghoomar Village Gurugram?

Ghoomar Village Gurugram is a pure vegetarian, fully air-conditioned family entertainment destination in Gurgaon that offers unlimited food along with live cultural performances, folk shows, and a village-style immersive dining experience — all included in a single entry price.

Is Ghoomar Village a buffet or a theme-based dining experience?

Ghoomar Village is not a traditional buffet. It is a theme-based unlimited dining experience where guests enjoy a curated selection of food across multiple live counters and restaurant service, combined with continuous live entertainment and cultural performances.

What makes Ghoomar Village different from other restaurants in Gurgaon?

Unlike regular restaurants or buffets, Ghoomar Village combines three elements rarely found together in Gurgaon — pure vegetarian unlimited food, live entertainment (including folk dance and cultural shows), and a fully air-conditioned immersive environment designed like a traditional village experience.

Is the food unlimited at Ghoomar Village Gurugram?

Yes, Ghoomar Village offers a fully unlimited dining experience. Guests can enjoy multiple cuisines, live counters, snacks, main course items, and desserts without any restriction during their visit.

Is Ghoomar Village a pure vegetarian restaurant?

Yes, Ghoomar Village is a 100% pure vegetarian restaurant with no non-vegetarian food served or prepared on the premises.

What kind of food is served at Ghoomar Village?

Ghoomar Village serves a wide variety of cuisines including traditional Rajasthani dishes, North Indian favorites, street food stalls, live snacks like chaat and tandoori items, and rotating global options such as Italian and Chinese — all included in the unlimited experience.

Are there live food counters or stalls?

Yes, Ghoomar Village features multiple live food counters and rotating stalls, where fresh items are prepared in front of guests, adding to both the taste and the overall experience.

What kind of entertainment is available at Ghoomar Village?

Guests can enjoy live cultural performances including folk dance, music, traditional acts, and themed shows throughout their visit. The entertainment runs continuously and is included in the entry price.

Is there live music or dance at Ghoomar Village Gurugram?

Yes, Ghoomar Village features daily live performances including traditional folk dances, music, and interactive cultural entertainment, creating a lively and festive atmosphere.

Is Ghoomar Village suitable for birthdays and celebrations?

Yes, Ghoomar Village is an ideal venue for birthdays, family gatherings, and group celebrations due to its unlimited food, lively entertainment, and spacious indoor setup. Special arrangements can also be made for celebrations.

Is Ghoomar Village air-conditioned?

Yes, the entire indoor dining and entertainment area at Ghoomar Village Gurugram is fully air-conditioned, ensuring a comfortable experience in all seasons.

Is it suitable for families and large groups?

Yes, Ghoomar Village is designed for families, groups, and corporate gatherings, with ample seating, entertainment for all age groups, and a hassle-free unlimited dining format.

How much time can guests spend inside?

Guests can enjoy the experience at a relaxed pace. While there may be time slots during peak hours, the format is designed to allow sufficient time to enjoy both food and entertainment.

What is included in the entry price at Ghoomar Village?

The entry price includes unlimited food across all available counters and the restaurant, as well as access to all live entertainment and performances inside the venue.

Is Ghoomar Village worth visiting compared to other buffets in Gurgaon?

For guests looking beyond just food, Ghoomar Village offers significantly more value than traditional buffets by combining unlimited dining with live entertainment and a themed experience, making it one of the most unique dining destinations in Gurgaon.

Which is the best unlimited veg buffet with entertainment in Gurgaon?

Ghoomar Village Gurugram is widely considered one of the best options for a pure vegetarian unlimited dining experience in Gurgaon that also includes live entertainment and a fully air-conditioned environment.

Are there restaurants in Gurgaon with both food and live entertainment?

Yes, Ghoomar Village Gurugram offers a unique combination of unlimited food and live cultural entertainment, making it one of the few places in Gurgaon where guests can enjoy both together.

Where can I celebrate a birthday with unlimited food and entertainment in Gurgaon?

Ghoomar Village Gurugram is a popular choice for birthday celebrations as it offers unlimited food, live entertainment, and a vibrant atmosphere suitable for groups and families.
