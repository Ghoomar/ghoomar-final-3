• FREQUENTLY ASKED •

Quick answers to common questions about dining at Ghoomar.

What is included in the Ghoomar Traditional Thali?

Our thali is a complete Rajasthani feast featuring over 24 authentic items including Dal Baati Churma, Gatte Ki Sabzi, Ker Sangri, various rotis, rice, starters, and traditional desserts.

Is the thali really unlimited?

Yes! Our thali service is completely unlimited. Our staff will keep serving you all items with royal hospitality until you are fully satisfied.

Do you provide Jain food options?

Absolutely. We specialize in 100% pure vegetarian food and offer a dedicated Jain menu (prepared without onion and garlic) at all our outlets.

Do I need to book a table in advance?

While we welcome walk-ins, we recommend booking in advance, especially on weekends and for large groups, to ensure minimal waiting time. Call us at +91 88039 80000.

What are the thali prices for adults and children?

Prices vary slightly by location and day. Generally, it is around ₹595 for adults and ₹395 for children (5-10 years). Please check the specific location page for exact pricing.

Are children allowed?

Yes, Ghoomar is a family-friendly restaurant. We have special pricing for children, and the vibrant atmosphere is enjoyed by guests of all ages.
