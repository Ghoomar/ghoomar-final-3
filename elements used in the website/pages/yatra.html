• FREQUENTLY ASKED •

Quick answers about how the Yatra works.

Is there an entry fee for Ghoomar Yatra?

No. Entry to Ghoomar Yatra is completely free. You only pay for the food you order and the cultural experiences you choose to join — nothing more.

How does the pay-per-use model work?

Walk in freely, browse the food counter and the experience menu, and pick whatever interests you. Each item is individually priced. Settle the final bill at the counter as you leave, or via UPI / WhatsApp.

Do I need to book in advance?

No advance booking is needed for individuals or small groups — just walk in. For groups of 10 or more, we recommend a quick WhatsApp message at +91 98102 11031 so we can keep your experiences ready.

What kind of experiences can I pay for?

Pagdi photo shoot, mehendi corner, Bhopa music session, puppet and folk shows, à la carte Rajasthani food, and handcrafted souvenirs. Each is offered individually so you can build your own yatra.

What are the timings?

Ghoomar Yatra is open daily from 12:00 PM to 11:00 PM. Cultural experiences and live performances run on a rotating schedule throughout the evening.

Is there a parking facility available at the venue?

Yes, we offer free basement parking for all guests at M3M Urbana, Gurugram.
