• FREQUENTLY ASKED •

Quick answers to common questions about your visit to the Village.

What exactly is Ghoomar Village?

Ghoomar Village is a 25,000 sq. ft. experiential space in Gurugram that brings the soul of Rajasthan to life. It features traditional welcomes, live folk performances, puppet shows, magic acts, cultural rituals, and an unlimited Rajasthani feast.

What are the timings for the Village experience?

The full Village Experience (including entertainment and food) runs from 5:00 PM to 11:00 PM every day. We also offer an Unlimited Lunch Thali from 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM (focused on dining).

Is the food included in the entry ticket?

Yes! Your entry ticket covers the complete experience — including the unlimited Rajasthani feast and all live entertainment, performances, and shows inside the village.

Is there a parking facility available at the venue?

Yes, we offer free basement parking for all our guests at M3M Urbana, Gurugram, ensuring a hassle-free arrival.

Is Ghoomar Village suitable for corporate events or birthday parties?

Absolutely. With 25,000 sq. ft. of space and nonstop entertainment, we are a premier destination for corporate outings, family celebrations, and large group events.

Do I need to book my tickets in advance?

We highly recommend booking in advance, especially on weekends and public holidays, to ensure availability. You can book via WhatsApp or call us at +91 98102 11031.
