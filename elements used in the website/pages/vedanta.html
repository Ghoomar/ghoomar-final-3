• ABOUT VEDANTA •

Vedanta by Ghoomar brings together premium ambience, thoughtful service, and a pure vegetarian multicuisine menu in the spiritual heart of Varanasi — perfectly placed for a relaxed meal after temple darshan or the evening Ganga Aarti.

Built for families, pilgrims, travelers, and celebrations alike, the space blends contemporary comfort with Indian warmth to create an elegant dine-in experience just off Luxa Road.

• WALKING DISTANCE FROM •

A short walk to the city's most-loved spiritual landmarks.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

~10 minutes on foot via Godowlia — ideal for a meal after darshan.

Dashashwamedh Ghat & Ganga Aarti

~12 minutes walk — book your evening table around the Aarti.

Godowlia Chowk

Just minutes away — the city's main heritage and shopping hub.

• SIGNATURE DISHES •

A taste of what Ghoomar is loved for — from a lavish Rajasthani thali to North Indian classics, South Indian, Chinese and continental favourites, all pure vegetarian.

Rajasthani Thali

Our signature lavish spread — dal baati churma, gatte ki sabzi, ker sangri, and traditional sweets.

Mirchi Vada & Pyaaz Kachori

Iconic Rajasthani street-style starters, served fresh and crisp.

Tandoori & Paneer

Paneer tikka, malai paneer, tandoori platters and rich North Indian gravies with fresh breads.

South Indian

Crisp dosas, idli, vada and filter coffee — comforting classics done right.

Chinese & Continental

Hakka noodles, Manchurian, pizzas, pastas and global comfort food for every age.

Desserts & Beverages

Traditional sweets, kulfi and falooda alongside hot and cold beverages.
