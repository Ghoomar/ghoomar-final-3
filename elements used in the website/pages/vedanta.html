• FREQUENTLY ASKED •

Quick answers to common questions about visiting Vedanta.

Is Vedanta pure vegetarian?

Yes — Vedanta by Ghoomar runs a strictly pure-vegetarian kitchen with no eggs.

How far is it from Kashi Vishwanath Temple?

About a 10-minute walk via Godowlia Chowk. Dashashwamedh Ghat and the Ganga Aarti are roughly 12 minutes on foot.

Do you take reservations?

Yes. We recommend booking ahead on weekends and around festival evenings. Call +91 99100 28098 or use the inquiry form above.

Is the menu family and kid friendly?

Absolutely. Our multicuisine menu has Rajasthani thali, North & South Indian classics, Chinese and continental favourites suitable for all ages.

Do you host group bookings or events?

Yes — family gatherings, pilgrim groups and special-occasion lunches are welcome. Reach out via the inquiry form for group rates.

What are your timings?

We are open Monday to Sunday, 7:00 AM to 12:00 AM. Breakfast is served from 7:00 AM to 11:00 AM.
